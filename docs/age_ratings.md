## age rating criteria

if none of these criteria apply, the game is rated _ages 3 and up_.
otherwise, the game will have the highest age rating of all
criteria that apply.

developers can set their game's age rating to a lower or higher
age rating than the highest-rated criteria suggests.
but they should only do it when they know what they are doing,
because setting an age rating too low is illegal.

### Player-made content

developers find it impossible to keep players from creating
inappropriate content in a game that the developers made to not have
inappropriate content.

if this inappropriate content is reasonably hard to find,
the age rating is raised only to _ages 13 and up_,
otherwise it could be raised up to _ages 18 and up_.

in games with player-made content, parents should ensure that their
children don't find the inappropriate player-made content.

intensity | age rating
---|---
player-made content | 13

### Online multiplayer

developers find it impossible to keep all players in online multiplayer
from being rude, so online multiplayer raises a game's
age rating to _ages 13 and up_.

in games with online multiplayer, parents should help their children
deal with rude players appropriately.

intensity | age rating
---|---
online multiplayer | 13

### Purchases

purchases don't raise the age rating because
whlie they are appropriate for _ages 18 and up_ only,
it's impossible for younger ages to make purchases —
if parents have made the right precautions, like keeping their
children from using the parents' credit cards.

also, parents can teach good purchase behavior by giving their child
a _very_ limited balance for spending in the game, like $2 a month.

intensity | age rating
---|---
one-time purchases | 3
consumable purchases | 3
subscriptions | 3
